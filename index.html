About Haryanvi Lunch

Our Story

Founded in 2026 by the Malik Family , Vansh Malik has been serving authentic Haryanvi cuisine for over three decades. Our passion for traditional recipes and fresh ingredients has been made us a beloved destination for food loves .

Every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail , using recipes passed down through generations of Haryana .
